Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is a beverage made by brewing black tea in milk and water and then sweetening with sugar.

Ingredients

(for 2 portions)

  • 4g ginger
  • 12g assam tea
  • 240ml whole milk
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 6 peppercorns
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 480ml water

Steps

  1. Wash the ginger and cube it.
  2. Put cardamom pods, cloves and peppercorns in a mortar. Lightly pound the spices with the pestle unitl they admit an aroma while still being whole pieces. (Alternative way: Crush the herbs with a black pan).
  3. Put water, ginger, cinnamon stick, crushed spices from the previous step, sugar and preferably other spices or aromas (i.e. petal of roses, saffron, fennel seeds, chinese anise, etc.) in a pot and bring it to boil. Boil the ingredients for 1 minute; bring the heat to a medium level
  4. As soon as the water no longer boils; add tea leaves and milk to the pot. Let it sit for 10-12 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Strain the liquid onto two cups to ventilate the tea.
  6. Serve the Masala Chai and enjoy!
