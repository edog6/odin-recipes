Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is a beverage made by brewing black tea in milk and water and then sweetening with sugar.

Ingredients

(for 1 portion)

  • 2g ginger
  • 6g assam tea
  • 120ml whole milk
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 6 peppercorns
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 480ml water
